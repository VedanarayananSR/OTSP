2Evidence gathered from source systems
3Subject interviewed (PACE-compliant)
4Disclosure schedule maintained (CPIA)
5Financial loss quantified

CT-03 · Optional
Financial Investigation
1Assets identified and traced
2Restraint or freezing orders sought
3Civil or criminal recovery route confirmed
4Proceedings commenced
5Recovery amount logged
CT-04

2HR / People team notified
3Disciplinary hearing managed
4Outcome: dismissal / warning

2Graded using 3x5x2 model

4Dissemination approved and logged
