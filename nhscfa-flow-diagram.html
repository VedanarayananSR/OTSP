How the system works

A simple flow of what happens from the moment a fraud is reported to the moment a case is closed.

Step 1 — Someone reports a fraud

Fraud Report Comes In

Reports arrive from multiple channels. System creates a case record instantly.

📞 Hotline 🌐 Online Portal 👤 Staff Tip-Off ⚙️ Automated Flag 🔗 API Feed
Step 2 — Is it real? How serious?
⚖️

Triage & Risk Scoring

An analyst reviews the report. Is it credible? Is it NHS fraud? How much money is at risk? The case gets a priority rating and is sent to the right investigator.

Critical High Medium Low
Opens one of these case types
Step 3 — The right case type is opened
🔍

Criminal Investigation

Used when the fraud could lead to prosecution. Every action is legally traceable.

1
Investigator assigned
2
Evidence gathered from NHS systems
3
Subject formally interviewed (PACE)
4
Disclosure schedule maintained (CPIA)
5
Decision: prosecute or not
📋

Disciplinary Investigation

Used when the outcome is employment action rather than criminal prosecution.

1
Evidence gathered
2
HR notified and engaged
3
Disciplinary hearing managed
4
Outcome: dismissal / warning
5
NMC / GMC referral if clinical
💰

POCA Financial Investigation

Chasing the money. Tracing stolen assets and getting them back.

1
Assets identified
2
Freeze orders placed
3
Recovery proceedings
🧩

Intelligence Record

Not yet a case — but worth keeping. Graded, sanitised, and shared carefully.

1
Intelligence graded (3x5x2)
2
Source protected
3
Shared to right people only
🛡️

Fraud Prevention & Risk

Fixing the gaps before fraud happens. RAG-rated and financially tracked.

1
Weakness identified
2
RAG rating assigned
3
Prevention impact measured
Criminal cases also produce
Step 4 — Going to court
⚖️

CPS Prosecution Bundle

When a case is ready for prosecution, the system generates the full bundle for the Crown Prosecution Service — every form, every exhibit, every witness, every piece of unused material listed and accounted for. Nothing missing. Nothing that could get the case thrown out.

MG
MG Forms
EX
Exhibits Schedule
WI
Witnesses
UM
Unused Material
Step 5 — What happens at the end

Possible Outcomes — All Recorded in the System

🏛️
Prosecution
Referred to CPS. Court dates tracked.
🚪
Dismissal
Employee dismissed via HR process.
💷
Recovery
Stolen money clawed back.
🛑
Prevention
System weakness fixed. Loss avoided.
📢
Regulatory Alert
NMC / GMC notified about a clinician.
❌
No Further Action
Unsubstantiated. Closed with reason.
